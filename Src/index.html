            TwilightMU* encompasses three White Wolf MUD roleplaying games, an informative wiki, and a interactive forum. We are a one-stop shop for World of Darkness roleplay through the ages. The three MUDs cover each time period from the Dark Ages to modern day, allowing for a wide array of options for roleplay. Players have the option of playing the role of a Medieval inquisitor or nobleman on Twilight Reconquista, a Victorian head of a secret society on City of Serenity the next, and a modern day lawyer or student activist on Bourbon and Blood thereafter. Whatever the desire for concept, we have a time period and mood to indulge, with an information compendium to support it.

      The story begins steeped in the darkness of Medieval Spain, in the year of our Lord, 1247. The world is dominated by the stigma and iron-fisted rule of the Church, but the shadows contain the demonic visages of Vampires, Werewolves, and the Infernal striving to withstand the dim light cast by the strong force of the Inquisition. Innocent mortals are caught in the middle, fought over and corrupted or protected, while the pending strain between the Christian force of Cordoba and the ever threatening flicker of Sevilla hovers ever in the backdrop. The Clans of Vampires themselves speak on the hierarchy of place, and though the Camarilla is not yet a whispered concept, the whim of the Prince and his Court guides the world of Cainites into a surviving mold against the evergrowing threat of supernaturally conscious humanity.

      Venice of the 1850's brings mankind to a tumultuous stage in development; what would later be called the height of nobility and civlization has begun to crest, drawing to a pinnacle where the only direction left is down either side. The Gothic beauty of the Victorian age is lit by lamplight, bringing Secret Societies, independent organizations composed of mortal hunters, the intricate Courts of Vampiric Kindred and the violent rabble of the Sabbat together, either alongside or directly against the now nearly bereft and completely underground Inquisitorial faction of the Church. While Venice recovers from civil war another war occurs beneath the surface; the battles of the undead rage onward against those who would oppose them, be they fellow Vampires or mortal bent.

      New Orleans in the year 2006 brings the player to a world completely familiar. The tired legends of werewolves and vampires are only stories, summoned up by overcreative childhood imaginations; Superstition is paid little mind and dismissed with a smirk by the majority of the populace. Mortals go about their lives unaware, attending school, going to work, paying their taxes, while in the confines of every alley, the shadow cast from each Cypress or mausoleum, and the darkness beneath every bed could contain evil unimaginable by the self-aware populace of humankind. The Inquisition remains, but is a mere candle-flame in comparison to the encroaching darkness, creeping ever inward as signs of Gehenna flicker in the awareness of those who know to look for them. The darkness has become more sophiscated, more deviant and more cunning, but still the battle between good and evil continues.

      The Twiki is our informational guide to everything White Wolf, World of Darkness and TwilightMU*, developed initially for those in need of anything from a quick reference guide to an all-out World of Darkness education in the form of an easy to use wiki. The Twiki is ever-growing and expanding, and is in its essence an encyclopedia of all information a person would need to know to play any of the three MUDs offered by Twilightmu*.

      In addition to the Twiki we have the forums, a more actively interactive method of information exchange, used both In-Character and Out of Character. Storyline information, IC news, Court exchanges and all other such methods of communication take place on the forums, alongside general chatting, fan fiction and expression of ideas. The majority of community interaction takes place on the forums.

      TwilightMU* as a whole is a massive arrangement of features intended to offer something for everyone, composed of a vast, talented community of roleplayers. We provide the information necessary to play our featured White Wolf systems, with a place to explore the possibilities of each in the era of the players' choosing. From the rawness of the Dark Ages, the classy refinement of the Victorian Era to the variety of of modern day, there is a TwilightMU* game for any player.
